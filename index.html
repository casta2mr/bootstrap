The Saxophone,

The most successful instrument design by Adolphe Sax, and a staple of many American musical traditions. There are 9 different instruments in the saxophone family, 5 of which are rarely even used!

Meet the family »

Brief History

The first saxophone was created in the early 1840s by Adolphe Sax, an instrument designer from Belgium. Since then, the sax has had great influence on musicians of all types. It began as an instrument used in classical ensembles and military bands, and gained popularity during the jazz age of the early 1900s. Today, saxophonists can be found playing in anything from wind and jazz ensembles, to rock and pop bands.
