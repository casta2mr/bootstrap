Alto Saxophone

Stats

  • Key: E♭
  • Range: D♭3-A5
  • Size: 70cm

Description

The alto saxophone is the most commonly played of all saxophones. Beginning saxophonists typically start on altos, as they are cheaper than tenors or baritones, but more practical than a soprano. Alto saxes see wide use in modern wind ensembles and jazz bands, but are often used in classic rock and pop songs. Jazz saxophonist Charlie Parker is believed by many to be the most influential alto sax player ever.
